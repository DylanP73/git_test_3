b

b bolds the text but it doesn't mean anything with the search engines

em

em stands for emphasis, this will make my text italic and meaningful with the search engines

i

i makes the text italic, but means nothing with the search engines. This just makes the text italic

li

li Child of 'ol' and 'ul' for creating list items

ol 'Ordered / Numbered' lists

ol adds an 'ordered list' with numbers instead of bullets

  1. Item
  2. Another Item
  3. Another Item
    1. List can also be added inside lists
    2. To be used as a child of the level above
    3. This is an unordered list within an ordered list
  4. etc

strong

strong bolds the text and tells the search enginges the word is important

ul 'Unordered' lists

ul adds an 'unordered list' with bullets and no numbers

  • Item
  • Another Item
  • Another Item
  • etc
